In my career as an educator, designer, and leader, I have had the privilege of working on a variety of projects, where my primary focus has always been enhancing the user experience (UX). I have had the opportunity to teach both online and in person to tens of thousands of students from various companies and teams.

Having encountered many common and specific issues in the field of product design, especially in education, I have developed not only a deep understanding of the challenges that UX faces but also a strong concern that encompasses not only constructibility (whether we can build it) but also the product and business strategy (whether we should build it). And, of course, how to tie all of this together with product management, which is highly present in any leadership role.

Furthermore, I am a creative catalyst and a passionate learner who enjoys combining various areas of knowledge to solve problems. In addition to my work as a people manager and Product Designer, I have a hobby in training my pet and I am also the author of two books focused on front-end (Sass and Bootstrap) and the UX Tips blog.
